Review
I wish I could give out half stars, because this place is just shy of 5-stars. The pools are fantastic. I love the variety of temperatures and the sizes. Where this place falls short is are the saunas and the sleeping area. The "sleeping" area is not conducive to sleep. It's a brightly lit, open area in the main locker room. The saunas are tiny for a spa this size, and the dry one is not nearly as hot as it should be. Aside from that, it's a nice facility. The Jjimjilbang and water park are the real key features, but as mentioned above, I'm just reviewing the Men's wet area.
